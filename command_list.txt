start - starts the bot and greets you
help - is absolutely useless, don't try this
status - sends a detailed status report from the host machine
events - get/stop notifications about events
epoch - shows your message's Unix epoch
whoami - sends you details about yourself
log - tails last 10 lines of log
quote - shows random quote from fortune
wikiquote - shows daily quote from Wikipedia

# deprecated
cercle - get/stop notifications for new Cercle events
thedreamers - get/stop notifications for new TheDreamers events
